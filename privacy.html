Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how giveQuest ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by Michael Posthumus, collects, uses, and protects information when you use our SMS-based quest gifting service.

1. Information We Collect

When you use giveQuest, we collect the following information:

  • Phone numbers — the gifter's phone number and the questee's phone number, provided at quest creation
  • Quest content — clues, challenges, messages, and reward details entered by the gifter
  • Quest responses — text replies, photos, videos, and audio clips submitted by the questee during a quest
  • Usage data — basic logs of quest creation and completion for service operation

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to operate the giveQuest service:

  • To deliver SMS quest messages to the questee
  • To notify the gifter of quest progress and step completions
  • To store and relay media submissions between questee and gifter
  • To display the final reward to the questee upon quest completion

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose beyond operating your quest experience.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own purposes. We use the following service providers solely to operate giveQuest:

  • Twilio — for SMS and MMS delivery
  • Supabase — for secure data storage
  • Mapbox — for generating location map images

Each provider is bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements. Your information is never shared with advertisers or marketing platforms.

4. Data Retention

Quest data — including phone numbers, clues, and media submissions — is retained for 90 days after quest completion, then permanently deleted. You may request earlier deletion by contacting us at the address below.

5. Opt-Out

Any message recipient can opt out of receiving further giveQuest SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. This will immediately halt all further messages related to their quest. To restart, reply START.

6. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information, including encrypted storage and secure API communications. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

giveQuest is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.

9. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the State of Michigan, without regard to conflict of law principles.
